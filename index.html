History of Eminem

Marshall Bruce Mathers III was born on October 17 1972. Professionally known as Eminem, has over 11 studio albums, 2 compilation albums, 1 ep, 57 singles and 22 featured singles as guest artist. He has 3 diamond album certifications, 50 platinum album certifications and 10 number one albums. His first albul was Infinite which was released in 1996 and the latest album to date is Music to be Murdered by: Side B, released in 2020. This makes him one of the most relevant artist on the long run, thus, he is well known for his longevity.
